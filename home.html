Welcome to Bashor's Team Athletics

Since 1944 Bashor's has been serving the needs of pacific northwest athletes of all ages. From little leagues to professional leagues we can help outfit your team in quality and style.




In April 2016 we moved to our new location. We are now located at 4812 N. Interstate Avenue in Portland Oregon. Located just a few blocks off of I-5, with easy access, and off street parking.




Our new location features a 3000sf retail space, almost 3500sf of production space, and 5500sf of warehouse so that we can stock most everything you need to get out onto the field or court.
